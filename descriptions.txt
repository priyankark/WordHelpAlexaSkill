Short description
Word Help is your best companion for word games.
 Find similar words, adjectives, nouns, rhyming words or look for particular patterns.

 Long description

 Word Help allows you to find synonyms, rhyming words, adjectives and look for other associations between words.
 The top five words are spoken and other possibilities are shown on a card in the Alexa app.

You can use this skill to assist you in word games like scrable or perhaps for writing prose and poetry or
 maybe just as a reference
